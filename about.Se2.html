ABOUT US

ABOUT US

The Barkery is a dedicated dog treat bakery that creates premium, all-natural snacks for dogs. Every treat is handmade in small batches using only safe, high-quality ingredients.

We believe in providing nutritious alternatives to commercial dog treats, using every ingredient with care and offering options like grain-free and limited-ingredient treats.

The Barkery focuses on creating healthy snacks that are both delicious and beneficial, so your pet can enjoy treats knowing they're getting the best. The Barkery lifeguards treats that are both delicious and nutritious, ensuring each bite contributes to your dog's wellbeing while making every pup's day brighter and tastier.

MISSION

The Barkery promotes a healthy lifestyle by being committed in creating nutritious and delicious dog treats from healthy grains and natural ingredients which will delight every pet's taste buds.

VISION

Become a leading brand for healthy and affordable pet snacks, ensuring every pet experiences the tail-wagging goodness of The Barkery delicious treats.
